Billie's Life

Billie a lo largo de su carrera cuenta con varios albumes como :Don't Smile at Me, When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?, Happier Than ever y Hit Me Hard and Soft.

"Don`t Smile at Me", lanzada el 11 de Agosto de 2017

Incluye las siguientes canciones: COPYCAT, idontwannabeyouanymore, my boy, watch, party favor, bellyache, ocean eyes, hostage y &burn.

Discografia: Darkroom

"When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?", lanzada el 29 de Marzo de 2019

Incluye las siguientes canciones: bad guy, xanny, you should see me in a crown, all the good girls go to hell, wish you were gay, when the party's over, 8, my strange addiction, bury a friend, ilomilo, listen before i go, i love you y goodbye.

Discografia: Interscope Records.

"Happier Than ever", lanzada el 30 de Julio de 2021

Incluye las siguientes canciones: Getting Older, I Didn't Change My Number, Billie Bossa Nova, my future, Oxytocin, GOLDWING, Lost Cause, Halley's Comet, Not my Responsibility, OverHeated, Everybody Dies, Your Power, NDA, Therefore I Am, Happier Than Ever y Male Fantasy.

Discografia: Interscope Records

"Hit Me Hard and Soft", lanzada el 17 de Mayo de 2024

Incluye las siguientes canciones: SKINNY, LUNCH, CHIHIRO, BIRDS OF A FEATHER, WILDFLOWER, THE GRATEST, L'AMOUR DE MA VIE, THE DINER, BITTERSUITE y BLUE.

Discografia: Interscope Recors y Darkroom.
